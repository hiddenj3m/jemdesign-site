Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 June 2026

JEM Design respects your privacy. This policy explains what information is collected when you contact JEM Design about photography, video or digital media services, and how that information is used.

Information collected

When you send an enquiry through this website or contact JEM Design on WhatsApp, you may provide:

  • Your name.
  • Your email address or phone number.
  • Project details, location, dates, budget or other information you choose to include.
  • Messages and booking-related correspondence.

How information is used

Your information is used to respond to enquiries, discuss availability, prepare quotes, plan shoots, provide booked services and keep basic business records.

Legal basis

JEM Design processes enquiry and booking information because it is needed to respond to your request, take steps before entering into a contract, provide services you book, and run the business responsibly.

Sharing information

Your information is not sold. It may be shared only where needed with service providers that help operate the website, process enquiries, manage communications, deliver booked work, or comply with legal obligations.

Retention

Enquiry and booking information is kept only for as long as needed for communication, service delivery, business records, tax, accounting or legal purposes.

Your rights

You can ask to access, correct or delete personal information held about you. Some records may need to be retained where required for legal, accounting or legitimate business reasons.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact JEM Design through the website enquiry form or WhatsApp on +44 7960 629992.
